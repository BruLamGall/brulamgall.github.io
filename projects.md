# VCC Model

## Robofest, 2026

When I was studying at Lawrence Tech, a teacher I had told me about the IGVC team, and I wanted to join. To be part of the team, I had to make a VCC (Vision Centric Challenge) model, which is a model that can detect and classify traffic signs in an image. I fine-tuned a YOLOv8 model to do so, and I achieved an accuracy of 95% on the test set.

### Python, PyTorch, YOLOv8, OpenCV, Computer Vision, Fine-tuning, Deep Learning
